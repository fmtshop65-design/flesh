Overview

RENUMB FLESH numbing dropper is a water-soluble liquid anaesthetic built for precision. It can be applied directly to a small, targeted area or mixed straight into the pigment — a method especially popular in permanent makeup, microblading and fine detail work where control matters more than coverage.

Each 15 ml bottle is EAC certified. Because the formula is water-soluble it blends cleanly with pigment without separating, letting permanent-makeup artists numb lips, brows and eyeliner zones while they work, with no thick cream to wipe away.

Key Benefits

The dropper is the specialist tool of the range — the answer for targeted application and for procedures where the numbing agent needs to travel with the pigment.

How To Use

STEP 01

Choose your method

Decide whether to apply the dropper directly to the area or to mix it into the pigment for in-procedure numbing.

STEP 02

Direct application

For targeted numbing, place a few drops onto the open area and let it absorb before continuing.

STEP 03

Mix with pigment

To numb while you work, add drops into the pigment cup and blend; the water-soluble formula integrates evenly.

STEP 04

Reapply with control

Top up drop by drop as needed. Because there is no cream layer, you keep full visibility of the work.
