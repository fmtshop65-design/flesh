Overview

RENUMB FLESH pre-procedure numbing cream is a professional tattoo anaesthetic cream formulated for application before the session begins. Applied to clean, intact skin and held under occlusion, it delivers deep, even numbing across the work area so artists can outline and pack with less client movement and fewer breaks.

Each 30 g tube is EAC certified and produced under Russian cosmetic-manufacturing standards. The cream is designed to have no effect on healing, making it suitable for long tattoo sessions, cover-ups, and sensitive placements such as ribs, inner arm, ankles and the back of the neck.

Key Benefits

Pre-procedure cream is the foundation of a comfortable session. It is the right choice whenever you need numbing in place before the needle touches the skin.

Deep, even coverage — one occlusive layer numbs the whole stencil area uniformly, not just the spot you start on.

2–3 hour working window — applied correctly, the effect holds long enough for full outlining and shading without re-prep.

Healing-safe formula — engineered to leave the skin's healing response unaffected so aftercare proceeds normally.

EAC certified, 30 g — compliant with Eurasian Conformity safety regulations; consistent batch quality for studios.

Ideal for sensitive zones — ribs, sternum, ankles, hands and necks become workable for both artist and client.

How To Use

STEP 01

Clean & prep the skin

Shave and disinfect the area. The skin must be clean, dry and intact — never apply to broken or irritated skin.

STEP 02

Apply a thick, even layer

Spread a generous, opaque layer of cream over the full stencil area. Unlike a moisturiser, it should not be rubbed fully in.

STEP 03

Cover with cling film

Occlude the cream with plastic wrap. The occlusion drives absorption and keeps the area warm and active.

STEP 04

Wait, wipe & begin

Leave for 30–45 minutes, then remove the film, wipe clean and re-apply the stencil if needed. Numbing holds 2–3 hours.
